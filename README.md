# About Us

Welcome to **Varanasi Tourist Guide**, your trusted partner for exploring the spiritual capital of India — Varanasi.  
Our mission is simple: to help every traveler experience the real essence of Banaras with comfort, clarity, and authenticity.

## Who We Are
We are a local Varanasi-based guiding platform dedicated to providing reliable information, curated tours, and genuine travel assistance for visitors from India and around the world. With deep knowledge of the city’s culture, ghats, temples, heritage sites and local traditions, we help you explore Varanasi like a true insider.
